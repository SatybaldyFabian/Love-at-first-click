Why are dating platforms so popular?

The dating platforms are popular, because it is simple to use, when a person is shy or has problems to speak about love, you can just chat with the person, send pictures and send voice messages. The dating platforms are also so popular because you are connected to the whole world.

Comparing two dating platforms.

For example, let us compare Tinder and Lovoo, they both are popular worldwide. In Tinder you swipe to the left or right. If you are interested in a person you just swipe to the right, the person is getting a message where it says that you are interested and want to contact the person, but when you are not, you swipe to the left. In Lovoo you have map, and it shows all people in a radius, you could be interested in. There is also a radar that shows you who’s in your local area. Lovoo Live allows you to share videos with members and watch theirs too.
